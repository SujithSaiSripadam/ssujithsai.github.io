Sripadam Sujith Sai

I am Sripadam Sujith Sai, a recent Chemical Engineering graduate (Class of 2025) from NIT Rourkela, with an exchange term at IIT Hyderabad.

My research interests are in computer vision, deep learning, and human–robot interaction.

I’ve had the opportunity to work with:

  • Prof. Kishalay Mitra at the Gokul Lab, IIT Hyderabad — on developing physics-informed neural networks for industrial crystallization and optimizing water networks in refineries.
  • Dr. François Brémond at the STARS Group, INRIA (France) — on building models to anticipate anomalies in spatio-temporal video sequences.
  • Prof. Hatice Gunes at the AFAR Lab, University of Cambridge — on creating multimodal graph-based models to evaluate mental wellbeing from robot-perceived human behavior.

Earlier, I worked on developing machine learning models for CO₂ capture and conversion.

Outside research, I enjoy playing badminton and brainstorming on creative technology applications (a few ideas are born from this).
